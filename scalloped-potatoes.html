Scalloped Potatoes

Description

Scalloped Potatoes are a classic dish with layers of potatoes and rich creamy cheese sauce on repeat! The ingredients are simple in these Scalloped Potatoes recipe but they are the perfect from scratch side dish to Easter, Christmas, or Thanksgiving!

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Salt & pepper
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Paprika

Steps

  1. Thinnly alice your potatoes.
  2. Create a roux with some milk and cheese whisked in.
  3. Layer the potatoes in a baking pan,then pour the sauce over top.
  4. Then you bake till you see the cheese start the brown.
  5. Enjoy!
