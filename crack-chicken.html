Crack Chicken Recipe

Description

Slow Cooker Crack Chicken has everything that is good and addicting in it! Filled with bacon, cheese, cream cheese, and ranch dressing mix.

Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs
  • Ranch dressing seasoning mix
  • Cream cheese
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Cooked bacon
  • Green onions

Steps

  1. Place all ingredients in slow cooker.
  2. Cook on low for 7 hours.
  3. Shred chicken.
  4. Serve with favourite side
